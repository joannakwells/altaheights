Manifesto Fragment

Wine for people who would rather be outside, later, longer.

Alta Heights belongs to the world of open roads, ridgeline weather, cold air, and dinner that starts after the trail is over. It should not feel overexplained.

Use more blank space than a normal ecommerce site. Let things sit apart from each other.
