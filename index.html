Why Mountain Wine

What we look for in the vineyards we choose.

The case for mountain fruit is simple: more freshness, more character, and more life in the glass.

01

Warm days and cool nights preserve acidity and natural freshness.

The Alta Heights Philosophy

Wine for people who'd rather be outside.

Alta Heights is inspired by California's mountains — trailheads, alpine light, cold mornings, long drives, and tables full of friends at the end of the day.

It is not a traditional winery dressed up as a lifestyle brand. The world comes first. The wine belongs to it.
