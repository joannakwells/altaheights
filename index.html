What is California Alpine Wine

Pure, alive, and inconvenient.

Mountain wine is the purist expression of terroir. Weathered-in-place soils create individualistic wines that are unlike anything else. The vineyards we work with are alive with the natural flora and fauna that surround them, cultivated by nature - not by machine. The hillsides are steep, the weather can be harsh, the hand-work is daunting. It's highly inconvenient and not optimized for everyone. As Ken Burnap said, we do it because we love it.

Mountain wines are more concentrated, more aromatic, more food-friendly. The tannins have texture rather than just grip. The acidity is fresh and real, not added. These are the qualities that only emerge from place, season, and honest winemaking.
